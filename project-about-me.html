Hi! I'm Aimée! I have recently moved to Bristol and am currently a student/trainee at Mayden Academy, an intense software development training programme based in Bath, and am very excited to be learning HTML, CSS, PHP, SQL and Javascript in the hope of pursuing a career in software development. Prior to this, I lived in Cornwall, working in payroll and administration and performing proofreading/translation tasks online. Alongside my university study, I used to tutor languages and mathematics and also worked in a kitchen, making pizzas.

Since January 2016, I have been undertaking a part-time degree with the Open University in Computing and IT, due to my increasing fascination for computing and technology. In July 2013, I graduated from the University of Sussex with a First Class honours degree in English. Software development neatly combines my love of languages and computing and I feel very fortunate to have been able to study both subjects! Growing up in Cornwall, I studied A Levels from 2007 - 2009 at Truro College and GCSE's from 2002 - 2007 at Penair School.

I have been playing piano for ten years and enjoy composing my own music. As an avid reader and writer, I hope one day to finish writing a novel, but so far have written some short stories, a graphic novella and other bits and pieces. Drawing/painting are likewise hobbies of mine, and I create album artwork for bands in my spare time.
